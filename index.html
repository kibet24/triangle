Triangle Tracker

This program will help you determine what type of a triangle you have.
Basically there are three types of triangles.


  • Equilateral Triangles.

    • These are triangles that have a common lenght in all of its 3 sides

  • Isosceles Triangles.

    • These are triangle that have only two sides sharing a common length measurement.

  • Scalene Triangles.

    • These are triangles that all the three side have their own independent measuremen.. also the sum of two sides of these triangles cannot be less than the lenght of the remaining side
